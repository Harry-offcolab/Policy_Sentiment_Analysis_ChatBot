🔍 About Sentiment Analysis Models

1️⃣ VADER (Valence Aware Dictionary for Sentiment Reasoning)

Type: Rule-based, Lexicon-based
Speed: Very Fast ⚡

Strengths:
✅ Excellent for social media, short texts, and casual language
✅ Handles emojis, slang, capital letters, and punctuation emphasis
✅ Lightweight, no GPU needed

Weaknesses:
❌ Cannot deeply understand complex sentence structures
❌ Not suitable for long texts or detecting sarcasm well

When to use VADER?
-- Quick results
-- Small datasets
-- Short sentences (Tweets, comments, reviews)
-- No GPU or high resources available

2️⃣ RoBERTa (Robustly Optimized BERT Pretraining Approach)

Type: Transformer-based, Deep Learning Model
Speed: Slower compared to VADER 🐢 (requires GPU for faster processing)

Strengths:
✅ Context-aware: Understands complex sentence structures
✅ Trained on large datasets
✅ Better at detecting hidden sentiment in professional or long texts

Weaknesses:
❌ Slower, needs more computing resources
❌ Requires GPU for best performance
❌ Overkill for short, simple texts

When to use RoBERTa?
-- Research-grade analysis
-- Complex, long-form articles, reports, professional documents
-- When accuracy and nuance are more important than speed
